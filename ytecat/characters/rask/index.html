Description

Rask is a winged Beastman hybrid — a bird of prey in humanoid form, hawk or eagle in bearing. Tall, lean, and powerful, built for flight and close-quarters precision in equal measure.

He has warm brown-to-dark skin with an earthy quality. His eyes are sharp and amber-gold — a predator's eyes that miss nothing. His nose is strong and slightly hooked, avian without being cartoonish. His hair is dark, kept close or pulled back. Large, dark-feathered wings fold close to his body when not in use, with feathering and scale-like texturing along his forearms and shoulder blades.

He carries himself with quiet strength. Not cold — restrained. Always watching, always assessing, never wasting a movement. The softness underneath is not something you see in a casual glance. It takes longer than that.

Gear

Dual daggers — carried at the hip or crossed at the back. Minimal armour — mobility first. Nothing that impedes the wings.
